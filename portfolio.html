    Bitmoji Royale

    As a final project in my first semester in college, we had to create a game using Bitmoji's of ourselves. This is a two player shooting game. The goal is to get your oppenent's health bar down by shooting them. You will run out of bullets if you do not collect the green amo packs. Collect health packs to increase your health bar. You can adjust the difficulty in single player mode. Use WASD to move Denno and shoot with "1" or "C." Use arrow keys to move JC and "." to shoot.

    DODGE RECT

    This game is a coded version of dodge ball. The goal is to dodge and hit your opponent with the rectangle that is being projected out of your character.
    If you are hit, the center line will move down and you'll have less space to move.
    If the center line moves is low enough, you lose.
    The start screen of this game was developed by Denno Louis and Clarence Emile.
    HOW TO PLAY
    Use arrow keys to move and press shift to blast rectangle to opponent.
