About Page

Denno Louis, born January 18th, 2001.

I graduated Cranston High School East still unsure about I wanted to do with my career. My cousin, Mozart Louis, a softwear engineer and app developer, started teaching me C++ during my time in high school. unfortunately he had to travel and I was left with no laptop and nor means of learning on my own with school, work, sports, and playing piano at my church.

I knew for sure that I wanted to create in my career. I love creating music, short skits, and ideas to solve problems. I finally saved enough to buy a laptop and decided to continue learning coding. Time flew by quickly as I stayed up past 3:00am coding. I simply enjoyed it. I found it fascinating how we could code exactly what we want.

I believe with Computer Science we can have new technological innovations. Everyone should learn how to code. Computer Science suits everyone. Artist who like to paint can code a program that gives them a canvas to paint on and save work. Musicains can code programs that organizes sounds. Doctors can have programs that detects viruses and illnesses. Computer Science is everywhere.
